About DBDesigns:

"It started as a gift for my mother's birthday. Now it's a NYC retail boutique that also holds popups, works with small businesses, and donates profits to charity."

  • Each piece of jewelry is hand-made and can be custom.
  • Necklaces come in various lengths
  • Stones, including emeralds, amethyst, and onyx can be customized onto pieces
  • Located at 112 West 17th Street | Open 7 days a week, Mon-Thur 11am - 7 pm, Friday and Saturday 11am - 8pm, and Sunday Noon-6pm

Who We Work With

We partner with other small businesses. In addition with non-profits, donating jewelry and store experiences to help raise money for causes.

Giving Back

As someone with celiac's disease, I understand this condition is very impactful so giving back to this cause and charity is extremely personal to me. In the past, a portion of our sales have gone to The Celiac Foundation, the Brooke Jackman Foundation, the American Cancer Society, Habit for Humanity amongst others.
